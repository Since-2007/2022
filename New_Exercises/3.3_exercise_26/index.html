In conclusion,  \(\small\overrightarrow{CB} \cdot \small\overrightarrow{PD}=- \small\overrightarrow{BC} \cdot \left(\small\overrightarrow{BD} - \small\overrightarrow{BP}\right)=- \small\overrightarrow{BC} \cdot \small\overrightarrow{BD} + \small\overrightarrow{BC} \cdot \small\overrightarrow{BP}=-①+②=0\), that is, CB⊥PD.
