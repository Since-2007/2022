Exercise  1： Let EDAC be a parallelogram. DBAC is a trapezoid with DC//BA and DC=2BA. BA=AC. Prove that EA⊥BC.
\(\because \) DC//BA and DC=2BA  \(\therefore \small\overrightarrow{AD}=2 \small\overrightarrow{AB} + \small\overrightarrow{AC}\).
\(\because \) EDAC is a parallelogram  \(\therefore \small\overrightarrow{AE}=2 \small\overrightarrow{AB} + 2 \small\overrightarrow{AC}\).
\(\because \) BA=AC  \( \therefore\small\overrightarrow{AB}^{2} - \small\overrightarrow{AC}^{2}=0.\)
In conclusion,  \(\small\overrightarrow{CB} \cdot \small\overrightarrow{EA}=- \small\overrightarrow{AE} \cdot \left(\small\overrightarrow{AB} - \small\overrightarrow{AC}\right)=- \left(\small\overrightarrow{AB} - \small\overrightarrow{AC}\right) \cdot \left(2 \small\overrightarrow{AB} + 2 \small\overrightarrow{AC}\right)=- 2 \small\overrightarrow{AB}^{2} + 2 \small\overrightarrow{AC}^{2}=0\), that is, EA⊥BC.
