\(\because \) D is the midpoint of BC  \(\therefore \small\overrightarrow{OD}=\dfrac{\small\overrightarrow{OB}}{2} + \dfrac{\small\overrightarrow{OC}}{2}\).

\(\because \) FAOE is a parallelogram  \(\therefore \small\overrightarrow{OF}=\small\overrightarrow{OA} + \small\overrightarrow{OB} + \small\overrightarrow{OC}\).
